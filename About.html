Who we are

Aishra Technofab Engineers uses an advanced, technological breakthrough solution for the design and construction of complex fast- track modular buildings. Featuring structural light gauge steel framing materials in lieu of traditional building materials, Light Gauge Steel Building is the new generation alternative construction solution to conventional building construction. Our steel framed buildings are delivered on time and in budget. Construction time is half of the traditional construction, with benefits such as superior quality, enhanced energy efficiency, design flexibility and improved site safety. This system is the perfect solution for service providers today.

Our building process begins with detailed computer model of building, which is engineered and virtually framed with cutting-edge design software. The framing is designed and manufactured into large simplified sections, each with pre-punched holes for mechanical and electrical runs. The entire framing package is assembled within a controlled indoor production facility, delivered directly to the site and installed by certified installation crew. We offer unparallel design flexibility and a wide range of cladding, roofing and glazing options. This means LGSF building will look no different from the traditional structured building.

We offer a wide range of modular building solutions like, low cost accommodation and relief housing to villas and resorts. Industrial or workforce accommodation, modular, portable or temporary housing and offices. Our clients include Indian Railways, Indian Oil Corporation, Hindustan Aeronautics Limited, AIIMS, Patna Municipal Corporation.
